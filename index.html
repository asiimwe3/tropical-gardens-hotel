About Us

A Serene Oasis in the Heart of Kyenjojo

Welcome to Tropical Gardens Hotel, an exquisite destination nestled in the heart of Kyenjojo, Uganda. We are delighted to provide a serene oasis where guests can escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and immerse themselves in tranquility, comfort, and warm Ugandan hospitality.

Tropical Gardens Hotel has a rich history that dates back several decades. Our journey began with a vision to create a haven of relaxation and rejuvenation amidst the natural beauty of Kyenjojo.
